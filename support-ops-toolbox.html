Support Ops Toolbox

A focused workspace for support leaders to design, monitor, and continuously improve their operations — built on top of AI Business Suite.

What's in the toolbox

The Support Ops Toolbox bundles together the most important automations and views for customer support teams:

  • AI macros that summarize conversations and suggest next best actions.
  • Intelligent routing rules that respect priority, skills, and SLAs.
  • Deflection flows that surface docs, FAQs, and AI answers before a ticket is created.
  • Health views for backlogs, first response time, and SLA risk.
  • Audit logs for every AI interaction in your support environment.

Try it from the main demo

On the main AI Business Suite page, the Support panel in the Live Demo simulates a typical support flow, including logging an issue and triggering routing and SLA tracking.

In a full deployment, these flows plug into your existing help desk stack, giving Support Ops granular control over where AI is allowed to act.
